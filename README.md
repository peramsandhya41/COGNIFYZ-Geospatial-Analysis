# COGNIFYZ-Geospatial-Analysis
# 📊 Data Visualization

This task focuses on creating visual representations of the dataset to better understand patterns, trends, and relationships between variables.

---

## 📌 Objective

The objective of this task is to:
- Visualize the distribution of restaurant ratings  
- Compare average ratings across different categories  
- Identify patterns and relationships between features  

---

## 📂 Dataset Features Used

The following columns were used for visualization:

- Aggregate Rating  
- Price Range  
- City  
- Cuisines  

---

## 📊 Visualizations Created

### 1. Distribution of Ratings

- A histogram was used to display how restaurant ratings are distributed  
- Helps in understanding whether ratings are skewed or evenly distributed  

---

### 2. Average Rating by Price Range

- A bar chart was created to compare ratings across different price ranges  
- Shows how pricing affects customer satisfaction  

---

### 3. Top Cities with Most Restaurants

- A bar chart was used to display cities with the highest number of restaurants  
- Helps identify areas with high restaurant density  

---

### 4. Feature Relationships

- Visual comparisons were made between features such as:
  - Price range vs rating  
  - Location vs restaurant count  

👉 These visualizations help uncover hidden patterns in the dataset.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Most restaurants have moderate ratings, indicating average customer satisfaction  

- Higher price ranges generally have higher ratings, suggesting better service and quality  

- Certain cities have a significantly higher number of restaurants, showing urban concentration  

---

## 💡 Conclusion

Data visualization plays a crucial role in understanding complex datasets. By using charts and graphs, it becomes easier to identify trends, patterns, and relationships that are not obvious from raw data.
